# Climate Job Boards

**Priority: 1** — Search every run before ATS or generic discovery.

These boards aggregate climate-specific roles earlier and with higher signal than general job aggregators. Roles here often appear days or weeks before they surface on LinkedIn or broad ATS sweeps.

| Board | URL | Why use it |
|-------|-----|------------|
| Climatebase | https://climatebase.org/jobs | Largest climate-job marketplace; strong startup + nonprofit mix |
| ClimateTechList | https://climatetechlist.com | Curated climate-tech companies and open roles |
| Terra.do Jobs | https://www.terra.do/climate-jobs/ | Climate career platform; analytics and ops roles common |
| GreenJobsBoard | https://www.greenjobsboard.com | Sustainability and environmental postings |
| WorkOnClimate Jobs | https://workonclimate.org/jobs | Community-driven; early-stage and mission-driven employers |

## How to use

- **Agent / manual:** Sweep these boards each discovery cycle for early-career, analyst, and data-science postings.
- **Scraper (today):** Not yet wired as automated feeds — listed here as **highest-priority human/agent sources**. See `../target_sources.md` for intended search order.
- **Signal:** Prefer postings that mention earth observation, geospatial, climate data, or sustainability analytics — not generic municipal GIS.

## Update policy

Add a board only if it is climate-focused and regularly lists technical roles. Do not duplicate general boards (LinkedIn, Indeed) here.
