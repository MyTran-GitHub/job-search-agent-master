# Climate Accelerators

**Priority: 3** — Startup discovery sources, not ATS boards.

Accelerators batch early-stage climate companies that often hire first analysts, scientists, and engineers in small teams. Watch cohort announcements and company pages — roles rarely appear on generic boards at this stage.

| Program | URL | Focus |
|---------|-----|--------|
| Y Combinator Climate | https://www.ycombinator.com/companies?tags=Climate | High-volume startup funnel; filter for data / EO / analytics |
| Elemental Excelerator | https://elementalexcelerator.com/portfolio/ | Climate hardware + software; Hawaii / global |
| Third Derivative | https://thirdderivative.org/portfolio | Climate tech commercialization |
| Activate | https://activate.org/our-fellows-companies | Deep-science → company spinouts |
| Creative Destruction Lab — Climate | https://creativedestructionlab.com/streams/climate/ | Science-based climate ventures |

## How to use

- Check new cohorts quarterly; add promising companies to your watch list.
- Cross-reference with `target_companies.md` before duplicating entries.
- When a company graduates to a public ATS, add a single line to `ats_boards.md`.
